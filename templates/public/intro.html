Independent Project — Currently In Development

JonesHQ Finance is a personal, independent project built from scratch and actively being developed. It is not a commercial product. New features are being added regularly and some areas may be incomplete or subject to change without notice.

If you encounter any bugs, broken pages, or unexpected behaviour, please visit the Help page and send an email — all issues are reviewed and addressed as soon as possible.

About This Project

JonesHQ Finance started as a way to replace scattered spreadsheets with a single, well-organised application tailored specifically to our household's needs. Rather than fitting into a generic budgeting tool, every feature here has been designed around real day-to-day requirements.

The platform is self-hosted and private — no third-party data sharing, no subscriptions, no ads. It runs entirely within our own infrastructure and is accessible only to invited household members.

Development is ongoing. If something isn’t working as expected or you have a suggestion, don’t hesitate to reach out via the Help page.
