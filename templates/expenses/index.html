Generate Monthly Reimbursements

This will create monthly reimbursement transactions for all submitted expenses.

Workflow:

  • Groups all submitted expenses by calendar month (YYYY-MM)
  • Creates single reimbursement transaction on last working day of month
  • Amount = total of all expenses for that month
  • Transaction type: "Expense Reimbursement" (Income)
Leave empty to process all months with submitted expenses
Generate Credit Card Payments

This will create automatic credit card payment transactions to clear expense charges.

Workflow:

  • Finds all months with expense reimbursements
  • For each month, groups credit card expenses by card
  • Creates payment transaction 1 working day after reimbursement date
  • Amount = total credit card expenses for that month
  • Transaction type: "Payment" (positive amount clears card balance)
Leave empty to process all reimbursement months
